Scatter and Histogram pickers expose the block's own outputs as Filter, Grouping/Color, Highlight, Size, Tab by, Tooltip, Label, and Additional-curve dimensions — matching how upstream metadata columns already behave. Users can now, e.g., color the Property Relationships scatter by Aromaticity while plotting Charge vs Hydrophobicity. X/Y defaults unchanged.
